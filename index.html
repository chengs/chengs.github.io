About Me

I received my B.S. (2011) and M.S. (2014) degrees from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, China. Now I am working in INRIA Saclay - Ile de France as a Ph.D degree candidate of Ecole Polytechnique, France. I have published 3 papers about wireless sensor networks in IEEE WCNC and WiMob. At present, I am working on understanding the correlations between user mobility and content demand, under the supervision of Aline Carneiro Viana.
